Detailed Description

Separatrix class used for separatrices from maxima to saddles and from saddles to minima.

Stores a separatrix between maximum and saddle (dimension 2) or between saddle and minimum (dimension 1). Origin and destination are stored, as well as the path (alternating sequence of 1-/2- or 0-/1-simplices) and a float for the separatrix persistence, that gives a value for the significance of this separatrix line.
