The function described in Robins et al.

2011, that returns a Morse Complex.

Loops over all critical edges and faces and follows the paths indicated by the gradient field that start at each critical cell. This gives the adjacencies of the cells and their paths between each other, giving us the Morse Complex as well as the Separatrices and thereby represents the surface in a reduced skelton.

Parameters
vert_dictThe dictionary containing all vertices.
edge_dictThe dictionary containing all edges.
face_dictThe dictionary containing all faces.
CThe dictionary containing all critical vertices, edges and faces.
V12The dictionary containing all pairings between edges and vertices.
V23The dictionary containing all pairings between faces and edges.
Returns
